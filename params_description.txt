##----------Parameter Description---------##
x: x coordinate
y: y coordinate

eet: lower boundary of fuzzy time window
et:  lower boundary of time window
lt:  upper boundary of time window
llt: upper boundary of fuzzy time window
d1:  lower boundary of fuzzy demand
d:   expectation demand
d2:  upper boundary of fuzzy demand
st:  service time

##----------Operation and Symbols---------##
0: depot
1: satellite
